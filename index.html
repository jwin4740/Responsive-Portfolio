About Me

My name is James Winkle. I graduated in 2010 from the University of Illinois with a degree in Biology. Upon graduation I engaged in biomedical research in the fields of Pharmacology, Reproductive Endocrinology, Psychiatry and Genetics. During that time, I coauthored an article published in 2013(5-HT2C paper). However, it became very clear the compensation that the academic research field offers is very difficult to get by. I came across and started a course on edX called cs50, and fell in love with it (I would highly recommend checking it out). So I am currently pursuing a career in web development.
